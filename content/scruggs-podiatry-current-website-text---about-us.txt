Dr. Ditra S. Scruggs, DPM

Podiatrist/Surgeon

 

Dr. Scruggs graduated from Lincoln University of with a B.S. in Biology/Pre-Med in 1984. She graduated from the Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine in 1991. After completing her podiatric medical residency at VA of Baltimore she entered private practice in 1993. She regularly attends educational seminars to keep up with the latest trends in podiatry.

 

She is a member of the American Podiatric Medical Association as well as the Maryland Podiatric Medical Association
